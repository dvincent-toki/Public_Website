Our Services

Comprehensive consulting solutions tailored to your real estate development needs

Market Analysis & Feasibility Studies

Comprehensive market research and financial feasibility assessments to evaluate development potential, including:

  • Demand analysis for proposed product types
  • Competitive market positioning
  • Absorption rate projections
  • Optimal product mix recommendations
  • Financial viability assessment

Pro Forma Modeling

Financial projection services including ad-hoc tailored reports to help you make informed investment decisions.

  • Multi year pro forma reporting
  • Long range revenue projections
  • Development cost estimations
  • Sensitivity analysis for various scenarios

Due Diligence Packages

Thorough investigation of all critical aspects of a potential acquisition including title, environmental, and entitlement risks.

  • Title and survey review
  • Environmental assessment
  • Entitlement risk analysis
  • Infrastructure capacity review

Zoning & Density Analysis

Expert evaluation of zoning regulations and potential variances to maximize allowable density and optimize land use.

  • Zoning code interpretation
  • Entitlement strategy development
  • Variance and exception analysis
  • Development capacity calculations

GIS/ESRI Mapping

Advanced GIS mapping and spatial analysis using ESRI software for site selection, market analysis, and development planning.

  • Site suitability analysis
  • Market area delineation
  • Competitive property mapping
  • Demographic visualization

1:1 Coaching

Personalized guidance for real estate professionals looking to enhance their development, underwriting, and investment strategies.

  • Financial modeling techniques
  • Development process guidance
  • Investment strategy development
  • Market analysis methodologies

About Me

Where planning expertise meets financial strategy — Daniel brings a rare combination of public sector experience and private market analysis to the table. From feasibility studies to investment-grade due diligence, our integrated approach delivers clarity, confidence, and results for any development project.

Professional Background

  • Leading planning and zoning initiatives as a city planner for the Town of Kiawah Island, home of the 2031 PGA Tour Championship. Oversee land entitlement processes, coordinate with stakeholders, facilitate advisory subcommittees, and develop ordinances aligned with the island’s environmental and development priorities.

  • Conducted financial underwriting for prospective land acquisitions, managed ongoing financial reports for active communities, and produced due diligence materials and feasibility analyses for projects across the Southeast at Kolter Homes.

  • Identified land acquisition opportunities through creative sourcing strategies, assembled comprehensive due diligence packages, and secured entitlements by presenting to local municipalities as an acquisition analyst at Dream Finders Homes.

Education & Credentials

  • Bachelor of Science in Financial Management from Clemson University
  • Member of the South Carolina American Planning Association board Housing and Community Development Chapter, Charleston Home Builders Association, and Urban Land Institute
  • GIS/ESRI Mapping Specialist
